SERVICE AGREEMENT
Revenue Uplift & Listing Optimization Services
MNW Holdings LLC d/b/a RevWhisper

This Service Agreement (this “Agreement”) is entered into as of the date of the last signature below (the “Effective Date”), by and between:

MNW Holdings LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, doing business as RevWhisper (“Provider” or “RevWhisper”); and the individual or entity identified on the signature page below (“Client”).

Provider and Client are each a “Party” and collectively the “Parties.” This Agreement is valid for one (1) property only.

1. Services

1.1 Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver the following done-for-you revenue uplift and listing optimization services for the Client’s short-term rental property (the “Listing”):

(a) Dynamic Pricing Management. Daily pricing adjustments informed by booking pace, competitive set movement, local event calendars, and platform-specific demand signals. Custom promotion deployment across all connected OTAs, including Airbnb weekly/monthly coupons, last-minute deals, and early bird discounts. Minimum stay optimization by season, day-of-week, and booking window. Gap night identification and tactical pricing adjustments. Monthly revenue reporting including ADR trends, RevPAR benchmarking, occupancy pacing, and actions taken.

(b) Listing Content Optimization. Algorithmic title optimization, keyword-dense description engineering, amenity audit and optimization, review response strategy, and seasonal content refresh cycles (minimum quarterly). All content optimized per OTA’s known indexing and search patterns.

(c) Photo Optimization. Professional photo editing (brightness, white balance, color correction, declutter retouching), photo ordering optimization by season and property type, visual description writing for all photo caption fields, photo gap analysis benchmarked against the competitive set, and photo quality scoring against known OTA algorithm signals.

(d) OTA Algorithm Strategy. Platform-specific algorithmic optimization across Airbnb and any additional OTAs connected to the Client’s account. This includes Instant Book strategy, calendar freshness management, response rate monitoring, Superhost/Guest Favorite qualification strategy, and new listing boost exploitation where applicable.

(e) Competitive Intelligence. Identification and ongoing monitoring of a competitive set of comparable listings per property. Tracking of competitor pricing, availability, reviews, and promotional activity to inform pricing and content strategy.

(f) Reporting. Monthly performance report delivered to Client showing revenue versus prior period, revenue versus market, ranking changes, actions taken, and upcoming strategy.

1.2 Execution Model

Provider executes all services directly. All pricing changes, promotion deployments, photo edits, description rewrites, photo uploads, review responses, and listing updates are performed by Provider’s team. Client is not expected to log into any platform to make edits.

1.3 Onboarding

Upon execution of this Agreement, Provider shall conduct an initial onboarding process for the Listing, which includes: a full listing health score audit, competitive set identification, guest avatar profiling, market demand baseline analysis, initial photo audit and editing pass, keyword and filter audit, baseline ranking position tracking, and setup of all monitoring systems. The onboarding process is expected to take approximately ten (10) business days from the date Provider receives all required account access.

1.4 Services Not Included

Provider does not provide property management, guest communication, cleaning coordination, maintenance services, or any on-site services. Provider does not handle payment processing, financial account management, or tax preparation on behalf of Client.

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date under one of the following term options selected by Client at signing:

  • 3-Month Plan: An initial term of three (3) months, after which this Agreement shall automatically continue on a month-to-month basis unless terminated in accordance with this Agreement.
  • 12-Month Plan: An initial term of twelve (12) months, after which this Agreement shall automatically renew for additional annual terms unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

2.2 Renewal

  • 12-Month Plan: Automatically renews for additional annual terms unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.
